Cancellation Policy

1. Refund Eligibility:
You are entitled to request a refund if cancellation is made within 24 hours of booking confirmation. After 24 hours, cancellations are non-refundable.

2. Dispute Process:
If you believe you have a valid reason for a refund beyond the 24-hour period, you may file a dispute by contacting our customer support team within 7 days of the scheduled appointment date.

3. Non-Refundable Situations:
- No refunds will be granted for cancellations made after the 24-hour grace period.
- No refunds for "no-shows" or missed appointments.
- Refunds will not be issued for dissatisfaction with service if no prior dispute or complaint was raised.

4. How to Cancel:
To cancel your appointment, please use the cancellation link provided in your booking confirmation email or contact our support team directly.

5. Rescheduling:
You may reschedule your appointment at no additional cost if done at least 24 hours before the original appointment time. Rescheduling requests made less than 24 hours prior may be subject to fees.

6. Refund Timeline:
Approved refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days to the original payment method.

7. Contact:
For all cancellations, disputes, or inquiries, please contact support@yourcompany.com or call 1-800-123-4567.

Thank you for your understanding and cooperation.
